Skirk

Skirk, the enigmatic Void Star, resides deep within the Abyss, far from the reach of light and mortal understanding. Detached from the surface world, she chooses solitude to avoid the curses born from bonds and death. Possessing a strength and perception beyond human limits, Skirk speaks only to those she deems her equals, wary of the miscommunication caused by vastly different worldviews. To her, life is a blessing, yet the truths of cosmic forces carry a weight not all are meant to bear. Her existence is a quiet defiance of fate, shaped by knowledge, silence, and the will to remain unseen.

Kiyoko Shimizu

Initially a quiet and reserved girl, Kiyoko Shimizu is deeply committed to her role as Karasuno's team manager, displaying a strong sense of responsibility and pride. Her dedication is evident when she, along with the other third years, hesitates to leave the club, showing how much the team means to her. Though not one to openly express her emotions, Kiyoko's care shines through subtle acts—like restoring Karasuno’s old banner to uplift the team’s spirit. According to Suga, such gestures are rare for her, but after Yachi joins, Kiyoko gradually opens up, revealing a warmer, more expressive side, often smiling and even cracking jokes to make her junior feel welcome.

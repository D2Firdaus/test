Skirk

Skirk, the enigmatic Void Star, resides deep within the Abyss, far from the reach of light and mortal understanding. Detached from the surface world, she chooses solitude to avoid the curses born from bonds and death. Possessing a strength and perception beyond human limits, Skirk speaks only to those she deems her equals, wary of the miscommunication caused by vastly different worldviews. To her, life is a blessing, yet the truths of cosmic forces carry a weight not all are meant to bear. Her existence is a quiet defiance of fate, shaped by knowledge, silence, and the will to remain unseen.
